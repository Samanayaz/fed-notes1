Seasons Of The Year

The following four seasons can vary significantly in characteristics of surrounding environment in the world. Let's take an overview of these four separate periods.

  • SUMMER
  • WINTER
  • FALL
  • SPRING

summer

Summer runs from June 1 to August 31

winter

Winter runs from December 1 to February 28 (February 29 in a leap year)

fall

Fall (autumn) runs from September 1 to November 30;

spring

Spring runs from March 1 to May 31
